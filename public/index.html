An Immitation of AI Profiling System

This is a group project about FACIAL RECOGNITION social experiment tool that analyses the faces of the user, collecting data about them to determine how the government use the information. The concept of this tool is to make the user a subject of the experience, exploring the subject of information and control through demostration of the amount data being collect by the user, how little say the user has on how much data is capture, and the consequences of using it in public.
